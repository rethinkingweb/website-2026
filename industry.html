Solutions

Manufacturing IT services, delivered at scale

We provide end-to-end IT solutions tailored specifically to the manufacturing industry. From ERP to IoT, we have got you covered.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System

An ERP system integrates various business functions such as inventory management, production planning, supply chain management, finance, and human resources over different aspects of the manufacturing process.

Manufacturing Execution System (MES)

MES solutions help in managing and monitoring the shop floor operations in real-time. They provide insights into production activities, track work orders, optimize scheduling, and monitor equipment performance.

Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) System

PLM systems manage the entire lifecycle of a product, from design and development to manufacturing and maintenance. They facilitate collaboration, improve control, and streamline product data and change management.

Supply Chain Management (SCM) System

SCM systems optimize the supply chain by automating processes such as procurement, inventory management, logistics, and demand forecasting. It reduces lead times, inventory costs, and improves supplier collaboration.

Internet of Things (IoT) Solutions

IoT technologies gather data from connected devices and sensors across the manufacturing process. This data can be used to monitor performance, track inventory, and optimize production workflows.

Data Analytics and Business Intelligence (BI) Tools

BI tools help manufacturers analyze large volumes of data collected from various sources. These tools provide valuable insights into production trends, quality control, resource utilization, and supply chain optimization.
