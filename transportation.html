Solutions

Transportation Logistics services, delivered at scale

We provide end-to-end IT solutions tailored specifically to transportation and logistics companies. From TMS to route optimisation, we have got you covered.

Transportation Management System (TMS)

TMS software helps in managing and optimizing transportation operations to streamline transportation processes, reduce costs, and enhance customer service.

Fleet Management System

Helps in monitoring and managing a fleet of vehicles, including real-time tracking, vehicle maintenance, fuel management, and driver performance monitoring.

Supply Chain Visibility Solutions

We provide real-time visibility into the supply chain, enabling companies to track shipments, monitor inventory levels, and collaborate with suppliers and partners.

Warehouse Management System (WMS)

WMS software facilitates effective warehouse management, including inventory control, order fulfillment, picking and packing, and shipment tracking to enhance warehouse productivity.

Mobile and Web Applications

We provide access to real-time information, enable online order tracking, facilitate digital document management, and enhance communication among stakeholders in the logistics process.

Route Optimisation Software

This software helps in planning and optimizing delivery routes based on factors such as distance, traffic conditions, delivery constraints, and vehicle capacity.
